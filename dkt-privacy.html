🚗 Aussie DKT Privacy Policy

Effective: March 15, 2026 | Last Updated: April 9, 2026

⚠️ IMPORTANT: Aussie DKT is an INDEPENDENT, UNOFFICIAL study tool. We are NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or approved by any government agency or road authority. For official information, visit your state's road authority website.

1. Who We Are

Aussie DKT is an independent, community-created study app designed to help Australian drivers prepare for their Driving Knowledge Test (DKT). We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a high-quality study experience.

2. Information We Collect

Locally Stored Data

  • Quiz progress and scores
  • Bookmarked or flagged questions
  • Your study session history
  • App preferences and settings

This data stays on your device. We do not upload it to our servers unless you explicitly create an account for cloud sync.

Technical Data

  • Device type and OS version
  • App crashes and errors
  • Feature usage (anonymized)
  • Approximate location (State/City only)

We collect this via Expo Analytics and Google Analytics to improve the app and fix bugs.

Contact Data

If you email our support team, we keep your email address only to respond to your inquiry. We do not use it for marketing or third-party sharing.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve the study app
  • Fix bugs and app crashes
  • Understand feature usage
  • Send app notifications and updates
  • Respond to support requests

4. What We Do NOT Do

  • ❌ We do NOT sell your data
  • ❌ We do NOT share your quiz answers with other users
  • ❌ We do NOT use data for targeted advertising
  • ❌ We do NOT sell your email address
  • ❌ We do NOT claim to be an official government app

5. Third-Party Services

To provide a quality experience, we use the following services:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Hosts app infrastructure in Australian region
  • Expo Analytics: Crash reporting and anonymized usage data
  • Google Analytics: Optional feature usage tracking
  • Apple App Store & Google Play Store: Distribution platform

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

6. Your Privacy Rights

As an Australian resident, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of analytics in App Settings
  • Know how your data is used

7. Data Retention

  • App usage data: Retained for 12 months
  • Crash logs: Retained for 30 days
  • Account data: Until you request deletion
  • Email inquiries: Until issue is resolved

8. Security

We take security seriously:

  • Data in transit uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Regular security assessments
  • Hosted on secure AWS infrastructure
  • Limited access by authorized personnel only

9. Children's Privacy

Aussie DKT is designed for users 16 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

10. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, data deletion requests, or general inquiries:

  • Email: support@jsmglobal.xyz
  • Website: https://jsmglobal.xyz
  • Location: Brisbane, QLD, Australia

We respond to privacy requests within 30 days.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as our practices evolve. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or email. Your continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

12. Important Disclaimer

Aussie DKT is an independent study tool. It is NOT an official government application. The content is based on publicly available Road Rules and official resources but is for educational purposes only.

For official DKT information, visit your state's road authority:

  • NSW: rms.nsw.gov.au
  • VIC: vicroads.vic.gov.au
  • QLD: tmr.qld.gov.au
  • And other state authorities
